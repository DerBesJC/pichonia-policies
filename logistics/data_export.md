# Data Export – Logistics
_Last updated: 2025-11-04_

## 1. Scope
This policy explains how users of the Logistics product can request an export of their data in a portable format.

## 2. What You Can Export
Subject to authentication and applicable law, we can export:
- Account profile (name, phone, email)
- Job/order history and related metadata
- Invoices/receipts and payouts (if applicable)
- Driver/vehicle assignments and activity logs (if applicable)
- Communications metadata (timestamps; message content only if retained)

## 3. Format
We provide machine-readable formats (CSV/JSON) and, for documents, PDF where applicable.

## 4. Identity Verification
We require sufficient verification (e.g., email/phone challenge, in-app verification) before fulfilling a request.

## 5. Timing
We aim to respond within 30 days, or as required by law. Complex requests may take longer; we will notify you.

## 6. Limits
We may redact or withhold data that:
- Would infringe on others’ privacy,
- Is subject to legal privilege, security concerns, or trade secrets,
- We are legally required to retain but cannot disclose.

## 7. How to Request
Send a request from your account email with subject “Data Export – Logistics” and include your full name and phone number for verification.
