# Privacy Policy – Dropt (Logistics App)
_Last updated: 2025-11-03_

Pichonia LLC (“Pichonia”, “we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Dropt mobile app and related services.  
**Contact:** info@pichonia.com · +1-202-670-7299 · 8 The Green STE B, Dover, DE 19901.

<!-- SECTION START: Scope -->
## 1. Scope
This policy covers the Dropt app and backend services that connect truck owners, drivers, and customers for logistics jobs.
<!-- SECTION END: Scope -->

<!-- SECTION START: Data We Collect -->
## 2. Data We Collect
- **Account Data:** name, email, phone; role (customer/owner/driver).
- **Business & KYC (if implemented):** business name, tax details, license/ID for drivers.
- **Location:** 
  - **Precise (foreground)** during active jobs for routing, tracking, and proof of service.
  - **Approximate** for browsing and discovery.
- **Device & Usage:** app/OS version, device identifiers, crash logs, analytics events.
- **Payments:** payer/payee identifiers, amounts, transaction references (processed by third parties; **we do not store full card data**).
<!-- SECTION END: Data We Collect -->

<!-- SECTION START: How We Use Data -->
## 3. How We Use Data
- Provide core functions (matching, dispatch, routing, notifications).
- Safety/fraud prevention; quality assurance.
- Payments and invoicing (through payment processors).
- Support communications (FCM/email/SMS), operational messages.
<!-- SECTION END: How We Use Data -->

<!-- SECTION START: Legal Bases -->
## 4. Legal Bases
- **Contract:** to provide the service you request.
- **Legitimate Interests:** safety, prevention of misuse, product improvement.
- **Consent:** precise location; marketing notifications where required.
<!-- SECTION END: Legal Bases -->

<!-- SECTION START: Sharing -->
## 5. Sharing
- **Processors:** Firebase (Auth, FCM, Crashlytics), Google Maps/Geocoding, **Stripe** (payments).
- **Counterparties:** limited job details between customers, owners, and drivers as needed to fulfill a job.
- **Legal/Safety:** to comply with law or protect users and the service.
<!-- SECTION END: Sharing -->

<!-- SECTION START: Retention -->
## 6. Retention
- Account & job records: kept while your account is active and up to **7 years** for tax/audit.
- Crash logs/analytics: typically **90–180 days**.
- We delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.
<!-- SECTION END: Retention -->

<!-- SECTION START: Security -->
## 7. Security
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS), restricted access, logging/monitoring.  
- No method is 100% secure; we work to continually improve controls.
<!-- SECTION END: Security -->

<!-- SECTION START: Your Rights -->
## 8. Your Rights & Choices
- Request access, correction, deletion, or portability where applicable.  
- Control permissions: location/notifications can be adjusted in device settings (note: service quality may be affected).  
- Contact: **info@pichonia.com**.
<!-- SECTION END: Your Rights -->
