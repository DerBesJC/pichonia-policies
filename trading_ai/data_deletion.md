# Data Deletion – Trading AI
_Last updated: 2025-11-03_

## 1. Scope
How Trading AI users can request deletion of personal data and what may be retained for compliance and security.

## 2. Who May Request Deletion
- **Account holders** with verified identity.
- Authorized representatives where legally permitted.

## 3. What We Delete
- **Account profile:** name, email, password hash, preferences.
- **Telemetry tied to your account** (feature usage, run counts) not needed for security investigations.
- **Optional integrations:** Revoked broker/data tokens and associated caches we hold.

## 4. What We May Retain (Required)
- **Billing & tax records** (up to **7 years**) per accounting/anti-fraud obligations.
- **Security/abuse logs** (**90–180 days**) to protect the service.
- **Backups**: encrypted backups may retain data up to **30–90 days** until overwritten.
- Minimal **aggregate/anonymized analytics** (non-personal) may persist.

## 5. Third-Party Processors Involved
If enabled for your account:
- **Stripe** (payments), market data providers (e.g., **Polygon.io**, **Alpha Vantage**, **Yahoo Finance**), and any **broker API** you connected.  
We will request/propagate deletion to these processors where applicable.

## 6. Identity Verification
- Submit from your registered email or via in-app signed request.
- We may ask for limited additional details (e.g., last invoice ID) to verify.

## 7. How to Submit a Request
- **In-App:** Account → Privacy → **Request Data Deletion**  
- **Email:** **info@pichonia.com** (subject: “Data Deletion – Trading AI”)  
Include **name** and **account email**.

## 8. Response & Timelines
- Acknowledge within **7 days**; complete verified requests within **30 days** (extensions communicated if needed).

## 9. Effects of Deletion
- Deletion ends access to the platform and removes your configurations, saved runs, and results. Exports made before deletion are your responsibility to store.

## 10. Appeals & Questions
Disagree with a decision? Reply to our outcome email or contact **info@pichonia.com** for a re-review.
