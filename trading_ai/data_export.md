# Data Export – Trading AI
_Last updated: 2025-11-04_

## 1. Scope
How Trading AI users can request their data in a portable format.

## 2. What You Can Export
Subject to authentication and applicable law, we can export:
- Account profile (name, email, MFA status)
- Strategy/configuration settings you provided
- Backtest/run metadata and outputs you generated
- Billing records and invoices (if applicable)
- Support tickets you submitted

## 3. Format
Machine-readable (CSV/JSON) and PDF for documents where relevant.

## 4. Identity Verification
We require sufficient verification (email challenge and/or MFA confirmation).

## 5. Timing
We target 30 days or the legally required window. Complex requests may take longer with notice.

## 6. Limits
We may redact/withhold content that reveals model internals, other users’ data, or protected security information.

## 7. How to Request
Send a request from your account email with subject “Data Export – Trading AI”. Include any relevant identifiers to help us locate your datasets.

**Contact:** info@pichonia.com
